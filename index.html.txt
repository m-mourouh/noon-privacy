  <h1>Privacy Policy</h1>
  <p class="updated">Last updated: March 03, 2026</p>

  <p>
    Noon ("we", "our", or "us") is a Quran and Islamic utilities app. This
    Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the Noon
    mobile application. Noon does not require an account and does not collect
    any personal information.
  </p>

  <h2>1. Information We Collect</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>
      <strong>Location data</strong> — We request your device location solely
      to calculate accurate prayer times and Qibla direction. Location is
      processed on-device and is never stored on our servers or shared with
      third parties.
    </li>
  </ul>

  <h2>2. Information We Do Not Collect</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>We do not require or collect any account information.</li>
    <li>We do not record or store any audio from your device.</li>
    <li>We do not track your usage behavior or sell data to advertisers.</li>
    <li>We do not share any information with third parties.</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>3. Data Stored Locally on Your Device</h2>
  <p>
    All app data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to
    any server:
  </p>
  <ul>
    <li>Bookmarked Quran verses</li>
    <li>Adhkar (dhikr) progress</li>
    <li>Reading goals and progress</li>
    <li>App settings (language, reciter, notification preferences)</li>
    <li>Cached prayer times and Hijri date</li>
    <li>Search history</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>4. Third-Party Services</h2>
  <p>The app communicates with the following external APIs to provide features.
  No personal data is sent to any of them:</p>
  <ul>
    <li>
      <strong>alquran.cloud</strong> — fetches Quran text and audio recitations.
    </li>
    <li>
      <strong>aladhan.com</strong> — fetches prayer times and Hijri calendar
      data based on your coordinates.
    </li>
  </ul>

  <h2>5. Permissions</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>
      <strong>Location</strong> — Used to calculate prayer times and Qibla
      direction. You can deny this permission; the app will ask you to enter
      your city manually instead.
    </li>
    <li>
      <strong>Audio</strong> — Used to play Quran recitations through your
      speakers. We do not record or access your microphone.
    </li>
    <li>
      <strong>Notifications</strong> — Used to send prayer time and adhkar
      reminders if you enable them. You can disable these at any time in your
      device settings.
    </li>
  </ul>

  <h2>6. Children's Privacy</h2>
  <p>
    Noon does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including
    children under 13. The app contains no age-restricted content.
  </p>

  <h2>7. Changes to This Policy</h2>
  <p>
    We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be
    reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the
    app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
  </p>
